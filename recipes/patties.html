Chickpea Patties Recipe

CHICKPEA PATTIES

DESCRIPTION

Chickpeas are a great food due to their energy density, fiber and protein content. They also have other micro-nutrients to keep you healthy from within.

Ingredients

  • 250 gm boiled chickpea
  • 250 gm sweet potato
  • 100 gm rice
  • 100 gm buckwheat flour

Steps

  1. Steam the sweet potato until tender and a fork can go through them. Remove the peel and keep aside.
  2. Rinse the rice under running water, transfer to a saucepan and cover with 1 cup of fresh water. Bring to a boil and set a timer for 15 minutes. Reduce the heat and boil the rice until all water has evaporated or the rice is soft and not crunchy. Set aside.
  3. Add cooked rice, cooked chickpeas and flour to a bowl and mash with a fork or use a food processor. Add any spices of your choice for seasoning and split into 8 portions and form patties
  4. Transfer to a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake in the middle of the oven at 392°F (200°C) for 20 minutes or until they begin to brown.
  5. Serve hot with a sauce or in a burger bun.
