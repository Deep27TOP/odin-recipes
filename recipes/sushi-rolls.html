VEGAN SUSHI ROLLS

The star ingredient of Sushi has always been raw fish, but in this recipe, we try to replace the star with our twist and use the combination which goes just as well to make Vegan Sushi

Ingredients

  • 200 gm rice
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 avocado
  • soy sauce
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice well. Add double the amount of water, add some seasoning and bring the water to boil.
  2. Then cover the lid and cook till the rice absorb all the water.
  3. Add the vinegar to the rice, mix well and let them sit.
  4. Cut an avocado, remove the seed and then cut into strips lengthwise.
  5. Similarly, cut a cucumber into 4 quarters lengthwise, remove the seeds and cut thin strips.
  6. Take some rice and spread them neatly on the nori sheets. Make sure to leave ~1 cm of space at one end of the sheets.
  7. Now place the avocado and cucumber strips on the rice towards the bottom.
  8. Now, carefully roll the nori sheets without spilling the rice.
  9. Cut the roll from the middle and then cut the two parts from the middle again.
  10. Sprinkle the Sushi Rolls with some white sesame seeds and serve with soy sauce.
