SUJI KA HALWA

DESCRIPTION

This fairly easy recipe can be made in minutes with just 5 ingredients. And will leave you asking for more.

Ingredients

  • Suji
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Nuts
  • Cardammom Powder

Steps

  1. Firstly, put some milk on heat.
  2. Then slightly roast the suji until its light brown and partially cooked. When the suji is a dark brown colour take it off the flame.
  3. Meanwhile, add some sugar and cardammom powder in the milk and stir to mix them well.
  4. Now, add this heated milk to the suji, turn on the flame and keep stirring the mixture until it reaches a thick enough consistency for your liking.
  5. Then, take it off the flame and garnish with some chopped nuts.
  6. Serve hot.
